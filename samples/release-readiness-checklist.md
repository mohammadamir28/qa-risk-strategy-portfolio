# Release Readiness Checklist

This checklist helps teams decide whether a release is ready from a quality and risk perspective.

## Change Awareness
- What has changed since the last release?
- Are all changes intentional and understood?

## Risk Awareness
- Which areas are most likely to fail?
- What is the impact if they do?

## Validation
- Have critical user flows been exercised?
- Were negative or edge cases considered?

## Data & Safety
- Is there any risk to customer or system data?
- Are rollback or recovery steps defined?

## Communication
- Are known risks clearly acknowledged?
- Does everyone understand the release decision?

A release does not need zero risk, but it must have **known and accepted risk**.
